## Vision

StayWithMe is a web-based platform designed to provide a supportive digital space for individuals who may be experiencing loneliness, emotional break-downs or overwhelming thoughts. In today's world, millions of people feel depressed and lonely often struggling with their trauma and panics alone. StayWithMe will focus on providing help when in need. It is "For the People" by "The People". 

The goal of the platform is to encourage emotional expression and reflection through tools such as anonymous confessions, personal journaling, and mood tracking. By providing a private and non-judgmental environment, StayWithMe aims to help users process their emotions and better understand their mental state.

Many people struggle with internal challenges but feel hesitant to speak openly about them. StayWithMe attempts to bridge that gap by offering a space where individuals can safely write, reflect and engage with their feelings.

## Motivation 

Mental well-being is often overlooked in digital spaces. While social platforms connect people externally, many individuals still feel internally isolated.

StayWithMe was created with the intention of:
	•	Encouraging emotional self-reflection.
	•	Providing a safe place to write thoughts anonymously.
	•	Helping users track emotional patterns over time.
	•	Creating tools that support emotional awareness.

This project explores how technology can support mental well-being through thoughtful design and accessible tools.

## Key Features

### Anonymous Confession Box

Users can write and submit thoughts or confessions without revealing their identity. This allows individuals to express difficult emotions without fear of judgment.

### Personal Journal

A private journaling system allows users to record thoughts, reflections, and daily experiences in a structured environment.

### Mood Tracker

The mood tracking feature allows users to log emotional states over time, helping them identify patterns and changes in mood.

### Emotional Reflection Tools

Users can explore prompts and reflection spaces designed to help process emotions and difficult thoughts.

### Authentication System

User authentication ensures that private journals and entries remain secure and accessible only to the individual user.

### Multilingual Support

The platform supports multiple languages to make the experience more inclusive and accessible.

 ## Running the Project Locally

### Navigate to the project folder:
cd stay-with-me

### Install Dependencies: 
npm install

npm run dev 

### Application will be run locally at: 
http://localhost:5173

  ## Ethical Considerations

Because this project focuses on emotional well-being, it is important to acknowledge that StayWithMe is not a replacement for professional mental health care.

The platform is designed as a supportive reflection tool rather than a clinical solution.

Future development will prioritize user privacy, ethical design, and responsible use of technology.
